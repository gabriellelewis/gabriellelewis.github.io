About

I'm a student journalist who's passionate about giving people a voice on issues they care about, stories that shape their livelihoods and the triumphs and challenges that face their communities. I'm currently a sophomore at the University of Maryland, College Park, majoring in journalism and criminology & criminal justice. I'm one of The Diamondback's copy chiefs and a staff writer for Stories Beneath the Shell. I've also worked at other campus publications such as Unwind Magazine and Her Campus Maryland. I'm most interested in covering stories on crime, social justice or underrepresented communities.
